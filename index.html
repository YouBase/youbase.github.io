Web3 for Everyone

The Cortex App is a decentralized notebook, web3 publishing tool, and wallet in one. It's the ultimate tool for creating, collaborating and transacting in the first complete and functional web3 stack that belongs to the users.

The Simplest Way to Move From Web2 to Web3

It all starts with your free NFT domain name. Claim yours at hmn.domains.

Web Address = Crypto Address

Blockchain links or "blinks": Every document inside Cortex is a human readable name that has a public/private keypair. When you link, you link to a web address and crypto address. They are the same.

Sharing and Collaboration

Keep things private for yourself, between you and one other, a group, or publish to the web to collaborate. Git-like features manage versions, history and even forking of your creations. Simple, decentralized and provable.

Linked Topics & Social Search

Notes have topics and links that allow you to share, search and contribute across your social sphere and globally to see what the people you care about are saying on a topic.
